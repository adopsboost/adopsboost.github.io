Dynamic flooring

Viewability optimization

Sequence and refresh with intent. Lift revenue by making ads more likely to be seen sooner.

How it works

1) Observe

We read Prebid events and slot context. We track bid CPMs, bid rates, win rates, and more.

2) Decide

We shape the next request. Which bidders to request, what floor to set, and how to time the requests.

3) Learn

We run multivariate tests. We log outcomes to GAM and your analytics. We keep what wins.

Why this matters: small, fast decisions compound over a session. You earn more without heavy migrations or lock-in.

  • Works with Prebid and Google Ad Manager
  • Client-side, low latency, small footprint
  • Export session and slot signals to analytics
  • Multivariate testing by design
  • Optimization by user, without user tracking.

Why “Rebounder”

In basketball, fewer than half of shots go in. Rebounds are how teams turn misses into points.

The web is similar. A page has many ad opportunities. Some are missed or underperform. Rebounder watches the play and adjusts in real time. If the last attempt was weak or off-target, we change the next one. The goal is simple. Fewer empty trips. More quality chances.

FAQ

Does this replace my wrapper?

No. Rebounder sits alongside your stack and guides it.

Will it slow my site?

No. Rebounder's script is loaded asynchronously and designed in a way where it won't slow down page load or ad load.

What data do you use?

Signals you already have such as Prebid events, slot context, header bidder behavior, and page metadata.

How do you measure success?

Our primary metric is Revenue, and we add targeting to Google Ad Manager to help us measure this using split testing. We track some other metrics internally such as bid rates, viewability, and speed for fine tuning.

What is the integration effort?

Add a script to the page, provide reporting access to GAM, and set up a few key values. That's it.
